Our Story

We started small, we kept it simple, and we haven't changed much since.

How It Began

Origin Story

Starbox opened in 2021 out of a pretty straightforward frustration, good coffee in the city was either overpriced, inconsistent, or both. We figured we could do better. We started with one location in Tagum City, just a small team, and no real plan to expand.


People kept coming back. That was enough for us. Three years later we have seven locations, but the way we run things hasn't changed much. Same suppliers, same standards, same approach to making sure every cup is worth what you paid for it.

What We Stand For

A few things we take seriously

We know where the coffee comes from

We buy directly from farmers we've met in person. We know their names, their farms, and their process. That's not a marketing line it just makes the coffee better.

You're welcome to stay

We don't design our spaces to move people along. If you want to sit for two hours with one coffee, that's fine with us.

No shortcuts behind the bar

Grind size, water temperature, extraction time. These things matter. Our baristas are trained to get them right, not just to work fast.

Community

We host local events, support neighbourhood artists, and run a community grant programme.

Less waste, not more

All our packaging is biodegradable. We work with suppliers who share that standard. It's not complicated, we just try not to make more of a mess than we have to.

Honest about what we are

We're a coffee shop. We're not trying to be a lifestyle brand or a movement. We just want to make something good and have people enjoy it.
